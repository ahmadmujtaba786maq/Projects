Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Eezy Booking respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website or mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and payment details.

  • Booking Information: Venue details, time, date, and preferences.

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and confirm your bookings.

  • Communicate important updates, reminders, and promotional offers.

  • Enhance user experience and improve our platform.

  • Ensure secure transactions and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. Data Protection Measures

  • Eezy Booking uses SSL encryption, secure payment gateways, and firewall-protected servers to keep your information safe.

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade user data to any third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We share limited data only with:

  • Payment processors to complete secure transactions.

  • Venue partners or service providers to confirm and fulfill bookings.

  • Regulatory authorities, only when required by applicable law.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login sessions.

  • Analyze user activity to improve performance and functionality.

Users can disable cookies through browser settings, though this may affect some features of the site.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.

  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.

  • Request details of how your data is used.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Effective Date.”

8. Contact Us

For privacy or data-related concerns, please contact us at:
📧 privacy@eezybooking.com

Refund Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

At Eezy Booking, we are committed to ensuring a seamless booking experience for our users. We understand that plans may change, and we aim to handle refunds and cancellations fairly and transparently.

1. Cancellations by Users

  • Users can cancel their bookings directly through their Eezy Booking account before the scheduled start time.

  • Full Refund: Bookings cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled time are eligible for a 100% refund, excluding payment gateway charges.

  • No Refund: Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled time, or missed bookings (no-shows), are not eligible for a refund.

2. Cancellations by Venue or Partner

  • If a venue, coach, or event partner cancels a confirmed booking due to operational or unforeseen reasons (e.g., weather, maintenance, or unavailability), users are entitled to a full refund or the option to reschedule at no additional cost.

3. Refund Processing Timeline

  • Refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days after approval.

  • Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Service fees, transaction fees, and partial booking deposits are non-refundable.

  • Promotional or discounted bookings may carry specific refund terms mentioned at the time of purchase.

5. Dispute Resolution

If you experience any issue regarding refunds or cancellations, please contact our support team:
📧 support@eezybooking.com
We strive to resolve all refund-related queries within 3 working days.

Frequently Asked Question

What are the benefits of becoming a member at Adelux Padel Club?

As a member, you gain exclusive court access, professional coaching sessions, priority in events and tournaments, special discounts, and become part of a vibrant and supportive padel community.

Can I upgrade my membership plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time. Any remaining balance from your current plan will be applied to your new package.

Is there a long-term contract required?

No. Our membership plans are flexible, with no long-term commitments. You can cancel or renew at your convenience.

What’s the difference between Group Training and Private Coaching?

Group Training sessions are held with multiple players and focus on skill-building and teamwork. Private Coaching offers personalized 1-on-1 training tailored to your individual goals.

How do I book a court with my membership?

Simply log in to your member account, select your preferred time and court, and complete the booking through our online reservation system.

Does membership include access to tournaments or events?

Yes, Standard and Elite members receive free or priority access to exclusive tournaments, leagues, and club events.

Can I freeze my membership temporarily?

Yes, you can pause your membership for up to 30 days per year. Just contact our support team to request a temporary hold.

Are gym and lounge facilities included in the membership?

These facilities are included with the Elite Membership. Other members can add them as optional extras.

Do you offer memberships for kids or students?

Yes, we offer discounted plans for students and juniors, including access to our training academy. Contact us for more details.

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership anytime through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer support team directly.
