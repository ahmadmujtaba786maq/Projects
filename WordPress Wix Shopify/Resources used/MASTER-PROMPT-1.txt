Master Prompt for Calculator App with Ads, Subscription, and Developer Controls
App Modes
AdMob Model (Default on first install)

Shows banner ads.

Shows interstitial ads every X minutes (developer can set time frequency in both Unity editor and developer login inside the app).

Basic calculator only.

Scientific calculator button is locked.

Scientific calculator can be temporarily unlocked for X minutes (default 30 min) by watching a rewarded ad — developer can toggle ON/OFF this feature in both Unity and developer login.

Notes & History:

Locked by default.

Cannot be unlocked by watching ads unless developer allows.

Dark/Light mode:

Locked by default.

Can be enabled by developer toggle in basic mode.

All feature unlock durations after watching ads are developer-controlled in both Unity and developer login.

Subscription Model (Upgrade Option)

No ads at all.

All features unlocked by default (Scientific calculator, Notes, History, Dark/Light mode).

Multiple subscription tiers allowed (developer can create and manage tiers in both Unity and developer login).

Subscription durations are developer-controlled for new subscribers (existing users keep their original duration).

Dark/Light mode toggle available in settings.

Apple-style button click sounds (can be turned ON/OFF in settings).

Notes & History always available (unless developer hides them via toggle).

Subscription button visible in:

Top of the calculator screen

Settings menu

Requires user account login so subscription is not lost after reinstall.

Subscription & Payment Integration
Uses Google Play Billing Library.

Subscription purchase flow:

User clicks “Subscribe” button.

Google Play handles payment securely.

App verifies active subscription status via Google Play Billing API.

Subscription tiers configured in Google Play Console (example: monthly, yearly, premium).

Subscription duration controlled by developer for future subscribers only.

Payment:

Google collects payment from user.

Deducts commission (15% for first $1M/year, 30% after).

Pays remaining amount to developer’s linked bank account via monthly payout.

Merchant account setup required in Google Play Console with bank verification.

Special Rules
Notes & History cannot be unlocked temporarily via ads unless explicitly enabled by developer.

Scientific calculator temporary unlock duration via ads is configurable.
