Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Can't find your answer? Call us — we're happy to chat.

Talk to a Bookkeeper →
What exactly does Rocket Bookkeeper do each month?
+
Each month we categorize all transactions, reconcile your bank and credit card accounts, update your chart of accounts, generate your P&L and balance sheet, and deliver a clean financial report to your inbox by the 5th of the month. You also get 24/7 access to your live dashboard.
How long does onboarding take?
+
Most clients are fully onboarded within 48 hours. We connect to your existing accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, Wave, or FreshBooks), clean up your chart of accounts, and handle any historical cleanup needed. You'll be off and running by the end of the first week.
Do I need to use QuickBooks or a specific software?
+
We work with QuickBooks Online, Xero, Wave, and FreshBooks. If you're not using any software yet, we'll recommend the best fit for your business and help you get set up at no extra cost. We do not support desktop versions of QuickBooks.
Will you also do my taxes?
+
We handle bookkeeping — not tax preparation. However, we keep your books tax-ready year-round and collaborate directly with your CPA or tax preparer, making their job (and your bill) significantly smaller. If you don't have a CPA, we can refer you to trusted professionals in our network.
What does it cost?
+
Our pricing is based on your monthly transaction volume and complexity, starting at $199/month for small businesses. We offer transparent, flat monthly pricing — no surprise invoices, no hourly billing. Schedule a free call and we'll quote you a fixed price within 24 hours.
Is my financial data secure?
+
Absolutely. We use 256-bit bank-level encryption for all data transmission and storage. We never store your login credentials — we use secure read-only bank connections. Our team is bound by strict confidentiality agreements, and we carry professional liability insurance for additional peace of mind.
What if I'm behind on my books?
+
That's actually our most common starting point. Many new clients come to us with months (or even years) of uncategorized transactions. We offer historical cleanup services to get you caught up quickly — and we'll quote you a fixed price for the cleanup before starting any work.
